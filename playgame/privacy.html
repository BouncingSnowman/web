Summary: This game can submit scores to an online leaderboard. If you submit a score, the name you enter and your score are stored and shown publicly on the leaderboard.

What we collect

  • Leaderboard name (the nickname you type, optional)
  • Score
  • Difficulty (e.g. NORMAL/HARD)
  • Timestamp (when the score was submitted)

Why we collect it

To display high scores and allow players to compare results on the online leaderboard.

Where it is processed

Scores are sent over HTTPS to our backend service (Supabase) for storage and retrieval. The leaderboard is publicly visible in-game.

Your choices

  • You can enter a non-identifying nickname (for example Pilot) instead of a real name.
  • If you do not submit a score, your score is not sent to the online leaderboard.

Data retention

Leaderboard entries may be retained for as long as the leaderboard feature is active, unless removed for moderation/maintenance reasons.

Contact

If you need a leaderboard entry removed, contact the game developer via the CrazyGames game page.
